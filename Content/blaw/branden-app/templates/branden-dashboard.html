About Chelsea

Located in London, England the Chelsea Blues are one of the top soccer teams in the English Premier League. I started following Chelsea in 2007 and have been faithfully getting up super early on Saturday and Sundays to watch them play. From devastating losses to Man United in the Champions League final to the dramatic win in the same competition 4 years later against Bayern Munich, I'm proud of being able to call myself a Blue.
